Welcome to the Westerlund 2 Multi-Wavelength Tour

This tour explores Westerlund 2 (WD2), one of the most spectacular young massive star clusters in our Galaxy. Located about 20,000 light-years away in the constellation Carina, this stellar nursery contains some of the hottest, most massive stars known. Experience this cosmic wonderland through different wavelengths and instruments, from Hubble's iconic visible-light imagery to JWST's revolutionary infrared views. Click "Start Tour" to begin exploring this stellar birthplace across the electromagnetic spectrum.
